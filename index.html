WE'RE THE FUTURE!!

THINK OF IT~ FOR THE YOUTH , BY THE YOUTH!

PURPOSE

Our purpose is to make the youth 'THINK OF IT and nurture their leadership qualities because we can foresee a nation run by them. It is our aim to involve the youth in activities that require responsibility and brings out their individuality. By incorporating values of teamwork, empathy, leadership, decision-making and social responsibility our aim is to propagate social work and make the youth realize that "It's not enough to be compassionate. You must act" in the words of Dalai Lama.

PROBLEMS & OPPORTUNITIES

Every opportunity presents its own set of problems. However those problems should never be the reason for us to give up on recognizing the potentialities. "THINK OF IT' sees that potential and the only solution is today's youth. It's an imperative task to recognize it and bring these gems to the forefront. The happiest people I know are those who lose themselves in the service of others-in the words of Gordon B. Hinckley, we see that giving back to the society is one duty that needs nothing in return yet it can be a fountain of peace to those who truly immerse themselves in it. 'THINK OF IT' provides three programs in order to spark a change throughout the nation which will cater to character-building and awareness for the masses. The solution is to lay out a path in front of the youth to walk collectively toward a better future.

WITH THE THREE FOUNDATIONAL PROGRAMS:

1. TOI-Youth Leadership Program

2. Reach to Teach Program

3. Raise a Dream Program

Our sole aim is to align the youth toward a meaningful future and nothing can set them across this path better than the ability to empathize, express, be intellectually equipped and confidence.

TOI-Youth Leadership Program

The "TOI-YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM' is, as is quite evident a program that aims to bring out the leadership qualities of the youth. Currently there are four projects that require the youth's attention and solutions Education, Food and Shelter, Sustainable Development & Environmental issues and Illegal & gender bias issues. They've the liberty to chose a project wherein lies their intrigue.

TOI-Reach to Teach

'When one teaches, two learn.' -in the words of Robert Heinlein, 'REACH TO TEACH' program is one of its kind because by being deployed in government schools the youth acquire the values of kindness and hard work by teaching it to students. 'REACH TO TEACH' program aims to provide the students with soft skills along with practical skills. It encourages artistic gifts such as dance, music, etc. and would provide them with mentors who can help them hone their skills even more. Furthermore through our tie-ups with institutions of dance, music and other forms of art we intend to give them a further push. Hence, when we have given our 70 per cent and the rest is fulfilled by an institution, a child will be 100 per cent ready for the competitive world.

NETWORKING

Networking, being an indispensable part of any set-up will be achieved through Youth Summits such as City Chapters, Youth Parliaments and Conferences across the nation.

Our current focus is confined to 2-3 cities and once we've left an indelible impact behind we move further to more cities in order to create a model which will be followed by them.

"THINK OF IT' stands to propel change at the ground level through various chapters held nationally.

All the managerial positions such as the City Chapter Vice President, Director, Operations Manager etcetera will be filled in by the 'Youth' based on their performance level which will be assessed and monitored closely. It will foster their leadership instincts when they'll lead city chapters and learn to communicate with others. There will be Youth Summits across the nation for the purpose of bringing together like-minded individuals. A hierarchy will be maintained for the systematic functioning and those who emerge as highly skilled individuals in city chapters will lead entire clusters in their cities. They will report on the activities covered under "THINK OF IT-YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM.
